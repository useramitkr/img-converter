There are no hard limits. You can upload as many images as you want. However, performance depends on your computer's available memory (RAM). Converting very large images or a huge number of files at once may slow down your browser.

WebP is a modern image format from Google that provides superior compression. This means you can often get significantly smaller file sizes than JPEG and PNG at a comparable quality level, which helps websites load much faster.
