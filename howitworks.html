How Image Flipper Works

A simple, secure, and speedy guide to our in-browser image conversion process. Your files never leave your computer.

1. Upload Your Images

Click the upload area to select images from your device, or simply drag and drop them. You can add multiple files at once. We support JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats.

2. Choose a Format & Quality

Select your desired output format (WebP, PNG, or JPG). For JPG and WebP, a quality slider will appear, giving you full control over the balance between file size and image quality.

3. Instant In-Browser Conversion

The magic happens instantly. Your browser's powerful built-in capabilities are used to convert the images right on your machine. No uploads, no waiting in a queue.

4. Download Your Results

Download each converted image individually, or grab them all at once in a convenient .zip file. The original files on your device are never altered.

Our Core Principles

Privacy First

Your images are never uploaded to a server. All conversion is done 100% on your local machine. We never see or store your files.

Blazing Fast

By avoiding server uploads and downloads, the conversion process is incredibly fast. The only limit is your computer's processing speed.

Batch Processing

Convert as many images as you need in one go. The "Download All" feature makes it easy to manage large batches of images efficiently.
